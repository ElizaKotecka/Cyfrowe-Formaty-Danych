About Baldur's Gate 3

Baldur's Gate 3 is a critically acclaimed RPG set in the Dungeons & Dragons universe. It offers unparalleled storytelling, intricate gameplay, and vast character customization options.

Playable Classes

Wizard

Masters of arcane magic, wielding devastating spells.

Fighter

Expert warriors excelling in melee and ranged combat.

Rogue

Stealthy and cunning, striking enemies where it hurts most.
